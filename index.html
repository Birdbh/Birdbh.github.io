Struvite Collection Research and Development

Research and Development Document

Over the course of 16 weeks, I worked as the team lead for a group of 6 engineers scheduling meetings, assigning tasks, and setting goals. We put together a 35 page proposal to reduce algea blooms in Manitoba waterways due to agricultural runoff. Our low-level model outlines the preliminary design that would be used on farms to reduce hazardous runoff.

Included in this researach and deveopment proposal were Solidworks Simulations and assemblies to assist in the implementation of this waste storage process.

As a final proof of concept, a miniture version of the reaction system was constructed with a pump and nutrient rich runoff. This assembly, report, and final design was submitted to the 2021 AquaHacking Western Canada Challenge.

AutoCAD Mars Rover Creation

Youtube Video of Motion Study

As part of a Solidworks design competition, I again led a small team of 8 members to create a replica of the Mars Discovery Rover. I recreated the central mast, building each of the 128 components myself using Nasa specification data.

The final design is made of well over 500 components and won 3rd place out of 87 college design teams.

Canadian Space Agency Microelectronics Testing

Proposal Document

In October of 2021 I started working on a brand new project for the Candian Space Agency High Altitude Balloon Challenge. In this challenge, I worked with a team to put together a research propsoal to test the efficiency of Silicon Semiconductor Designs to Mitigate Single Event Upsets (SEU) in Microcomputers.

Our propsosal was submitted January 2021 and was accepted by the agency. We are now in the development stages of the experimental design with launch set for August 2022. Above is the software rendering of the experimental appartus.

This project has required a high level of management and clear work breakdown structure, as well as consistent communication among the many complicated interconnecting tasks.
